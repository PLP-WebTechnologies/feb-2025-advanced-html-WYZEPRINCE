Design Best Practices

  1. Clarity: Design elements should be clear, concise, and easy to understand.
  2. Consistency: Establish consistent design patterns throughout the app to create a cohesive user experience.
  3. Accessibility: Design with accessibility in mind, ensuring equal access to information and functionality for all users.
  4. Emotional Connection: Create an emotional connection with users through empathetic design and engaging visuals.
  5. Flexibility: Design elements should be flexible and adaptable to accommodate different user needs and preferences.
